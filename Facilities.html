Gymnasium

This gymnasium is a multi-sport court that includes

basketball nets, volleyball nets, soccer nets, and balls.

This gymnasium is perfect for any schools or clubs

who don't have a space to practice. For more

information, check out our informations page.

Auditorium

This auditorium provides a versatile space

for performances, assemblies, concerts, and

special events. With a large stage and seating

area, it can accommodate both small and large

gatherings.
